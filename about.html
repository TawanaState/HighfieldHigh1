About

This application aims to guide people through Highfield 1 High, from those who would like to know where the Library is, to those who would like to know when and where the next Health Club meeting is being held. Through the use of certain products from Google and Microsft, it has a robust database and authentication structure, thus helping to keep your information private and providing the best user experience.

Features

Locate Friends

Finding your friend has never been easier!
Find anyone in UZ who has their location turned on.

Go Places

A detailed map with directions for anyplace you want to visit around the campus.

Plan Events

Set a location for an event, and make it public, such that anyone can see.

AI Chat Bot

A personal guide through Highfield 1 High. Giving answers to any of your questions.
